Microbiology (children’s story) Project
Dec 2023
Adobe Premiere Pro
This Video presents a children's story focused on microbiology, introducing concepts related to COVID-19 and E. coli in a fun and engaging way. It simplifies complex scientific ideas to help young viewers understand how these microbes affect health, their role in the body, and how we can protect ourselves from infections. Through colorful visuals and storytelling, it aims to educate kids about important topics like germs, hygiene, and disease prevention.
Battle of Abraham Project
Apr 2022
Adobe Premiere Pro
This Video covers a quick overview of the Battle of the Plains of Abraham, a pivotal moment in Canadian history. Taking place in 1759, this battle marked a significant point during the Seven Years' War between British and French forces. The brief video explains how the British victory led to the fall of Quebec and had lasting effects on North American history.
